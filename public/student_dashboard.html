Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my internship details?

Click on "Internship Details" in the sidebar or use the quick action button. Fill out all required fields and upload your offer letter.

When should I submit my internship report?

Submit your report after completing your internship. Make sure to include a detailed summary and rate your experience.

What file formats are accepted?

We accept PDF, DOC, and DOCX files. Maximum file size is 10MB.
